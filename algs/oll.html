OLL Algorithms

OLL is the 3rd step in the CFOP method for solving the 3x3, it stands for Orient Last Layer. The beginner version, 2-Look OLL, has two main sets of algorithms. First, EOLL to orient the edges, then COLL, to orient the corners.
